Our Grandparents & Us

Connecting families across generations

Technology is a powerful tool. But taking into account that kids are living in their teenage world of social media accounts, and grandparents are demanding technological instruction to impress their friends, it can feel as if technology is tearing your family apart! Kids are at risk of seeing themselves as unloved and ignored while their elder counterparts feel confused and left behind.

Now, there’s a solution.

Centered around our passion for grandparents and those around them, the Grandparents & Me website is built to satisfy grandparents’ need-to-know feelings through fun and interactive activities made to be enjoyed with their grandkids! Meanwhile, we provide parents guidance and comfort through weekly blog posts as kids get the chance learn about conditions and diseases and how to create the best kind of memories with those they love.

Technology doesn’t have to be the barrier between our generations. With Grandparents & Me, no elderly or kid will be left behind!
