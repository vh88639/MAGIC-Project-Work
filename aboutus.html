Who Is Behind the Our Grandparents & Us Website?

By Our Grandparents & Us founder Victoria H.

MAY 13, 2020

The picture above shows a picture of my Oma and me. I will always appreciate the valuable experiences that my mom cultivated for my brother, my Oma, and me.

My main inspiration, on the other hand, were my experiences with my grandpa, whom we called Opa. When I was in 1st grade and when my brother had just been born, our Opa suffered a stroke. After that, he was never the same. The energetic, strong, know-all grandpa suddenly became a bedridden, sickly, speechless man with a diaper. Despite the boundaries, my super mom still managed to forge a loving and strong relationship between my brother, me and Opa. After years of valiantly fighting his illnesses, Opa passed away last year.
